Abstract

The Construction Properties (CP) taxonomy contains OWL properties to describe general aspects of constructions, construction components, spatial zones, damages, etc. The main taxonomy is structured using RDFS subsumption axioms, starting from either [contax:property_OP](https://w3id.org/con-tax#property_OP) or [contax:property_DP](https://w3id.org/con-tax#property_DP).

The namespace for CP terms is https://w3id.org/con-tax/properties/cp#

The suggested prefix for the CP taxonomy is cp

The Turtle version of the CP taxonomy is available at https://w3id.org/con-tax/properties/cp/cp.ttl
The RDF/XML version of the CP taxonomy is available at https://w3id.org/con-tax/properties/cp/cp.rdf
The JSON-LD version of the CP taxonomy is available at https://w3id.org/con-tax/properties/cp/cp.jsonld
The N-Triples version of the CP taxonomy is available at https://w3id.org/con-tax/properties/cp/cp.nt

The CP taxonomy was created by Mathias Bonduel (KU Leuven) during his PhD research (2017-2020) funded by the Research Foundation Flanders in the form of a Strategic Basic Research grant (grant agreement 1S65917N).
